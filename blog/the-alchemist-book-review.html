The Alchemist Book Review

By Tausif Raja / Dec 7, 2020

A novel by the Brazilian author Paulo Coelho that was first published in 1998.Originally written in Portuguese ,it become a widely translated international bestseller. The Alchemist follows the journey of an Andalusian boy named Santiago.

Early in his journey ,he meets a old king named Melchizedek , who tells him to sell his sheep , so as to travel to Egypt, and introduces the idea of a personal Legend.

And, when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.

- The Alchemist

Coelho wrote The Alchemist in only two weeks in 1987. He explained that he was able to write because the story was already written in his soul.
