Pather Panchali

By Tausif Raja / Dec 15, 2020

Books feed our hearts. Reading is a passion of ours. Pather Panchali is a novel that people relished so far. It is an immortal creation of Bibhuti Bhusan Bandopadhay. Man's eternal journey amidst ups and downs of life and his search for truths of life is the main theme of the book. Harihar and Surbajaya, a rustic couple pass their days in dire distress


But poverty cannot mar their minds. They dream of a rosy future. Their children Apu and Durga, are the children of nature .Apu and Durga reminds us of our childhood. Durga dies early. Harihar with his family quites the village. This shows that life is stationary. This novel impels us to love humans and nature. The book works magic upon our minds.
